About Us

The YB (YABAWA) Oil entreprise is located at Limanti ward, behind old prison/kaliari, within maiduguri. we specialise in pure palm oil sales and services at an affordable prices. we source our products right from our suppliers in Sapele LGA, Delta state. our product (palm oil) is an essential nutrient containing Vitamin A (Retinol) which improves eye sight and enhances tissue growth and development. it is also rich in unsaturated fatty acid that provides calories and energy for metabolic activities in the body cells. it is very viscous and purely red in colouration, the characteristic of pure palm oil from genuine source.
